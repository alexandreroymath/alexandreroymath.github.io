Research Interests

My research lies in arithmetic geometry and arithmetic dynamics. My topics of interest include:

  • The study of degeneracy of archimedean objects toward non-archimedean objects by using hybrid Berkovich spaces.
  • In particular, the study of degeneracy of complex rational maps towards non-archimedean rational maps by studying compactifications of the space of rational maps.

Lately, I have been interested in studying how a algebraic group action extends to the hybrid compactification of a variety.
